Scene 3: UFO Sightings Through the Years

In the previous two scenes, we explored how the aspect of culture and the concept of collective perception contributes to the narrative of UFO sightings. The scatter plot shown in this scene further supports those two ideas.

The chart shows the frequency of sightings over the course of a century. And as can clearly be seen, the number of sightings increased exponentially after 1990, the dawn of the World Wide Web - when the internet became available to the masses.

As more and more people report seeing unidentified flying objects, these accounts create a powerful cultural narrative that shapes how subsequent witnesses interpret their own experiences.

While the recent spike in interest in UFO phenomena is certainly worth paying attention to, it is important to keep in mind the cultural state of the current age as well. It is entirely possible that some of the sightings of UFOs are in fact extraterrestrial in nature. However the sheer number of sightings alone cannot be pointed to as the smoking gun evidence for the arrival of aliens.
